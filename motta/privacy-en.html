Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

"Motta?" ("the App") respects your privacy. This Policy explains the information the App handles.

1. Information stored on your device

Data such as scenes, belongings, and check history is, in principle, stored only on your device. It is not sent to the developer's servers.

2. iCloud sync (optional)

Only when you enable iCloud sync in Settings is your data stored and synced to your own iCloud (Apple's servers). This is managed in accordance with Apple's privacy policy, and the developer cannot view its contents.

3. Calendar (optional)

When calendar integration is enabled, the App reads the titles of the day's events solely to suggest relevant belongings scenes. Event contents are not stored or transmitted externally.

4. Location and weather (optional)

When weather integration is enabled, your current location is used solely to obtain weather from Apple's WeatherKit. Location information is not stored or transmitted externally.

5. Photos (optional)

If you add a photo to a belonging, the image is stored only on your device (and in iCloud when iCloud sync is enabled).

6. Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you before you leave home. Notification contents are processed only on your device and are not sent to the developer.

7. Sharing with third parties

The App does not sell or provide users' personal information to third parties. It does not use any advertising SDK or analytics SDK.

8. Deleting your data

Uninstalling the App deletes all data stored on your device. If you have enabled iCloud sync, data on iCloud can also be deleted through Apple's standard procedures.

9. Contact
